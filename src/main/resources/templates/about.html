Our Journey & History

Founded in the heart of Rusizi District, G.S Gihundwe has been a beacon of knowledge for decades. From its humble beginnings as a small community school, it has grown into a leading institution known for academic rigor and strong moral values.

Our journey is marked by a commitment to the "Made in Rwanda" spirit of education—empowering the next generation of leaders to contribute to our nation's Vision 2050.

Our Spiritual Identity

Protestant Values & Faith

At G.S Gihundwe, we believe that academic brilliance must be anchored in strong spiritual foundations. Our school fosters an environment of fear of God, integrity, and mutual respect.

Umucyo Choir

The heart of our worship. Umuco Choir brings together talented students to lead the congregation in praise, fostering a spirit of joy and unity through sacred music.

GET (Group Exangelic Theatre)

Creativity meets faith. Through powerful drama, sketches, and spoken word, the GET sub-family translates biblical truths into relatable performances that inspire positive character change.

League (Bible study group)

Rooting student lives in the Word. Regue focuses on deep Bible study, fellowship, and prayer, ensuring our students grow into responsible citizens with a Christ-centered worldview.
